Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how My TODO App ("the Application") handles your information. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Data Collection and Storage

My TODO App is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be absolutely clear: we do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or task information on our servers.

All of your tasks and any preferences you set within the application are stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device.
  • We have no access to your tasks, ever.
  • There is no user registration, login, or database on our end.

The benefit of this approach is maximum privacy for you. The implication is that if you clear your browser's data, use a different web browser, or switch to a different device, your tasks will not be synchronized and will appear empty on the new browser/device.

2. Cookies and Tracking

My TODO App does not use cookies for tracking or any other purpose. As we do not collect or transmit user data, there is no need for such tracking technologies.

3. Third-Party Links

This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by My TODO App. While our application may contain links to other websites (e.g., social media links on the contact page), we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every website that collects personally identifiable information.

4. Children's Privacy

My TODO App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
